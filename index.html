MY JOURNEY

Hello and Welcome! I'm Jordan, a Leicestershire based Web & Front-End developer / UI-UX designer. To read a little about my story so far hover over ♥ My Journey ♥.

After some years of retail, I decided to set my sights on bigger things for myself and my daughter. I went to university studying Computer Science which began my love for functional-based programming.

I decided to further my knowledge and share my skills as much as possible by working towards my teaching degree in Computer Science. My university lecturers were a big inspiration for this, the values and passsion of technology and software is so vast and everyone deserves a chance to delve into it, if they so please.
Unfortunatly settling into my teaching position, a few years later I found myself at a serious uphill chapter. The Pandemic had hit, schools had closed and humanity sort of stood still but onwards and upwards we go!

My passion for design and creation is at an all time high. I am always looking to develop my skills further and learn something new. Currently I am learning REACT, Mongo DB, Node, AWS Cloud, as well as producing digital and traditional art commisions.
